About Utila Island Cleanups

Utila Island Cleanups is dedicated to protecting the natural beauty and ecological health of Utila through weekly beach cleanups.

We ensure collected waste is transported off the island and processed responsibly by Recycle Honduras.

Why It Matters

Clean beaches are essential for sea turtles, the endemic Swamper iguana, and overall ecosystem balance.

Removing trash prevents it from returning to the ocean and harming marine life.
